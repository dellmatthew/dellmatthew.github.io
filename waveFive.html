Fifth Wave Emo

Modern Emo

Fifth wave emo or “Post emo” is still currently evolving and changing. The wave starts around 2020 and runs still today. Similarly to fourth wave emo, fifth wave accomplishes more of the same things, the focus had shifted back to it’s first and second wave roots. There are some new bands like Glass Beach, which they have this energetic and whimsical take on the genre. At this point, all of the subgenera and distinct sound have been blended together and what is considered “emo music” can really be about anything. Today, there are mixes of bedroom pop, shoegaze, or experimental rock within the genre. Fifth wave emo is still evolving, but at this point has mostly devolved and blended together with other genres and is just “Emotionally Driven Music”.
