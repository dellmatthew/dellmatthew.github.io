About Me

Why Global Popular Music?

I needed a few more credit hours and, while browsing the list, I landed on this class and thought it sounded interesting. Great to see I was right! Why did I choose Global Popular Emo for my media project? I think that the genre of Emo started in the 1980s and continued to evolve and change, ultimately going mainstream. This is fascinating to me. I really love the sound of the music and, while I don’t adopt key aspects of Emo culture, I enjoy the music very much. Personal favorites include American Football, Sunny Day Real Estate, MCR (of course), Lou Reed, and Fugazi. I think the sound comes from the Punk/Hardcore scene.

Who Is This Media Project For?

This project is for the internet, where it has the most access to people globally. I wanted my media to be in this form because of its accessibility, which is one of the most important traits in what makes something both “Global” and “Popular.” A website also allows me to organize all the information needed to understand the genre and culture.
